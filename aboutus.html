Robert Susa

About Us

Robert Susa is a distinguished business leader with a lifelong dedication to supporting inventors and advancing innovation. As President and Owner of InventHelp, he has guided the company with integrity, transparency, and a commitment to excellence for over three decades. Under his leadership, InventHelp has become a trusted partner for inventors, offering comprehensive services that help transform creative ideas into tangible presentations. These services include patent referrals, prototype development, and professional marketing materials, all designed to empower inventors at every stage of their journey. 

Robert’s leadership philosophy is centered on honesty, accountability, and open communication. He believes that providing clear guidance and fostering informed decision-making creates a strong foundation for lasting success. By prioritizing ethical practices and continuous improvement, he ensures that every inventor experiences both support and clarity throughout the innovation process.

Beyond his professional achievements, Robert is dedicated to philanthropy and education, reflecting his commitment to opportunity and growth for others. His approach combines experience, empathy, and strategic insight, making him a respected leader in the invention services industry. Through his work, Robert Susa continues to champion innovation, ethical leadership, and the realization of visionary ideas.
